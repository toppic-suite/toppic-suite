Instructions to interact


Input Data :

  • Please enter Peak & intensity with space seperated list or tab seperated list
  • Please enter Mass & intensity with space seperated list or tab seperated list
  • Please enter protein sequence

C and N Terminus :

  • select any of "a","b","c" as N Terminus
  • select any of "x","y","z","z°" as C Terminus

Way to change Mass Shift :

  • Add Fixed Ptm by clicking , provide "acid" on the left box and "mass shift" on the right box and click on submit
  • Remove Fixed Ptm by clicking
  • Change the mass shift of individual acids by clicking on the acid and entering the mass shift in the box provided and click on "ok"

Interacting with the spectrum graph :

  • Scroll down to view the graph
  • X-Axis represent the Mass and Y-Axis represent Intensity
  • The vertical lines indicate the peak list entered and the circles represent the distribution calculated
  • Zoom the X-Axis by keeping the mouse on X-Axis and scrolling
  • Zoom the Y-Axis by keeping the mouse above X-Axis line and scrolling
  • Drag the X-Axis by draging the graph at any part
